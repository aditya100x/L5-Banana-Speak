About Minions

Minions are small, yellow, mischievous creatures introduced in the 2010 film Despicable Me. Created by Illumination Entertainment, they are known for their love of bananas and their childlike behavior. Originating as a comedic sidekick in the Despicable Me franchise, they gained popularity and starred in their own 2015 spin-off.
